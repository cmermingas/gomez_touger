In Memoriam

Gloria Gonzalez Cooper
April 18, 1963 - March 28, 2019

It is with great sadness that we mourn the loss of our invaluable team member Gloria, who passed away March 28, 2019. She will be remembered for her commitment to our clients and her unwavering dedication to her profession, her family, and her friends. She will be greatly missed by all of us at Gomez & Touger, P.A. and we dedicate ourselves to honoring her by continuing her work with the same loving and passionate spirit she shared with us all.
